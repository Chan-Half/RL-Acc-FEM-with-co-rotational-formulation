Basic Keyboard Operations: ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Sheath Control: Numpad up, down, left, right, and io; sheath moves backward, forward, left, right, forward, and backward (fem_run586-618).

Insertion Unit Control: wsadqe; insertion unit moves backward, forward, left, right, forward, and backward (fem_run494-580).

x; rotates the foreground coordinate system around the axis, controlling the camera and keyboard/handle operation in the up direction (fem_run553).

z; twists the foreground, applying force; generally avoid pressing this button.

Reset Button: 1; restores all positions. It's best to return the handle to its original position before pressing this button, otherwise it will follow the handle when moving again (fem_run452).

Adjusting Initial Pose: Press 1 to reset, press 2 to enter initial pose adjustment, then wsadqe and ikjluo translate along the x, y, and z axes to the corresponding foreground and rear points respectively. After adjustment, press 3 to return to basic operations. Key bindings: 123, wsad, qe, ikjl, uo, fem_run642-735

Exit: esc, or close the window by pressing the upper right corner, fem_run445-451


Empty Run: Press space or 'r' to run only FEM, leaving everything else unchanged. This is equivalent to rerunning collision detection and displacement calculation. It's best not to press 'r'. (fem_run633-639)

Gamepad Control: ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Operation unchanged: fem_run347-443

Mouse Control: ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Left Mouse Button: Rotate around the camera's origin. Right Mouse Button: Translate along the camera's x and y axes. Middle Mouse Button: Translate along the camera's z-axis, zooming in and out.

Code Location: fem_opengl_draw 915-953, response speed and axis adjustment are in fem_opengl_draw367-369
